Let's Play Battleship!

Instructions:
Click an empty square and try to sink all 5 of your opponent's ships in the least amount of turns as possible.
Opponent's Ships:
  • Carrier: 5 squares
  • Battleship: 4 squares
  • Destroyer: 3 squares
  • Submarine: 3 squares
  • Patrol Boat: 2 squares
